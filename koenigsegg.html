Koenigsegg Jesko - $3,000,000

Specifications:

  • Engine: 5.0L Twin-Turbo V8
  • Horsepower: 1,280 HP (Regular Fuel), 1,600 HP (E85)
  • 0-60 mph: 2.5 seconds
  • Top Speed: 300+ mph

Price Range: $2,800,000 - $3,000,000

Legacy: The Jesko is named after Jesko von Koenigsegg, the father of the company's founder. It's designed to be the ultimate track car and hypercar.

Production: The Jesko has been in production since 2020.

  • Top Speed: 251 mph

Price Range: $2,000,000 - $2,500,000
